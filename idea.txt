There are several features that you can consider adding to an e-commerce platform for artisans to sell their products:

User-friendly Interface: A user-friendly interface is critical for the success of your platform. The interface should be easy to navigate and should allow users to quickly find the products they are looking for.

Product Catalog: The app should allow artisans to create and manage their product catalog. This can include product descriptions, images, pricing, and inventory management.

Sales Management: The platform should allow artisans to manage their sales, including tracking orders, managing payments, and handling shipping.

Demand Forecasting: Demand forecasting can help artisans to better manage their inventory and ensure that they are producing the right amount of products.

Quality Checks: Automatic quality checks can help to ensure that only high-quality products are listed on the platform.

Sentiment Analysis: Sentiment analysis can help artisans to understand customer feedback and make improvements to their products.

Next Recommendation Actions: The platform should provide recommendations to artisans based on sales data, customer feedback, and other relevant factors.

To implement these features, you will need to work closely with artisans to understand their needs and preferences. You can also use data analytics tools to analyze customer behavior and identify trends in sales and product demand. Additionally, you may need to integrate with third-party tools for quality checks, sentiment analysis, and other features. Finally, it is important to ensure that your platform is secure and that customer data is protected.
